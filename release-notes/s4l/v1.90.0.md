# Release Notes

### Version 1.90.0 · March 05, 2026

Better help. Better documentation. Better organization.

This release focuses on improving how users **get support and find information** when working on the platform. Whether you have a quick question, need guidance, or want to explore the technical details, help is now easier to access than ever.

From now on, when you open the **Support Center**, you're not only connected to the App Team — a **chatbot is also available to assist you instantly**. It can help answer common questions, guide you through features, and point you to the right documentation while the team steps in whenever needed.

And speaking of documentation: the **User Manual has been completely redesigned**, expanded, and reorganized to make it far easier to navigate and discover information.

---

## ✨ Highlights

### 🤖 Support Center: Now with Chatbot Assistance

The **Support Center** has a new team member.

In addition to the Support Team, a **chatbot is now available to assist you directly from the Support Center**. The chatbot can help answer common questions, guide you to relevant documentation, and assist with navigating platform features.  

This means you can often get help **instantly**, without needing to wait for a response. And if your question requires human support, the Support Team can seamlessly step in to continue the conversation.

### 📚 A New and Improved User Manual

The **User Manual has been completely redesigned** to provide a clearer, richer, and more user-friendly documentation experience.

The new manual includes:

- **Expanded content** with more explanations and practical guidance
- A **more intuitive structure**, making it easier to find what you need
- Clearer descriptions of platform features and workflows
- **API references**, allowing developers and advanced users to explore and integrate with the platform more effectively

The goal of the new documentation is to support both **new users discovering the platform** and **experienced users looking for deeper technical details**.

If you haven't visited the manual recently, it's definitely worth another look.

### 🏷️ Easier Tag Management

Managing tags just became much more straightforward.

You can now **add or remove tags directly through an improved UI**, making it faster to organize projects without interrupting your workflow.

The updated interaction simplifies how tags are handled and removes a few of the small frictions that previously made quick edits less convenient.

Whether you're creating a new project or organizing existing work, tags are now easier to manage.
